RobiNet

is a toy network simulator that allow you to inspect individual packets from a pcap file or captured on an Ethernet device, fake networked machines able to stand a communication with some real equipment, or why not a whole fake network of fake hosts, that you can automate and benchmark.

It’s free software so you can freely use, copy, modify and re-distribute it under the terms of the Affero GPLv3.
